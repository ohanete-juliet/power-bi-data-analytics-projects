 Project Overview
This dashboard analyzes passenger survey responses to understand the key drivers of dissatisfaction and identify actions that can help improve overall customer satisfaction for Dano Airlines.

Business Objective
To identify:
- Why passengers are dissatisfied
- Which customer segments are most affected
- What service areas should be prioritized for improvement

Tools Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- Excel

Key Insights
- Overall satisfaction rate is low, indicating a critical need for service recovery.
- Economy class and personal travel passengers show the highest dissatisfaction levels.
- The largest dissatisfaction drivers include:
  - Online Boarding
  - Seat Comfort
  - In-flight Entertainment
  - WiFi
  - Cleanliness
- Delays are strongly associated with lower satisfaction.

Recommendations
- Prioritize improvements in high-impact service areas such as online boarding and comfort.
- Focus recovery strategies on Economy and Personal travel passengers.
- Improve delay communication and offer service recovery gestures.
